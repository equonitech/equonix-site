Sensors & Actuators

The key focus of IoT is data and it is crucial that this data is accurate. At Equonix, we are experts in Sensors and Actuators development which ensure that accuracy. We create a smart connect between people, machines and processes for unified functioning. With IoT, we take the evolution of sensors and actuators to a completely different level.

Advantages of Equonix Sensors & Actuators

  • We combine multiple discipline capabilities and deliver technologically advanced sensor and actuator ecosystem from concept to prototyping, testing and full production.
  • We develop innovative, low power and wireless sensors to integrate into IoT system and solutions, while our actuators are the perfect solution for simple, safe and clean movement with accurate and smooth motion control.
  • Our sensors and sensor systems, which are produced via qualified industrial processes result in increased accuracy, decreased size and cost, and the ability to measure or detect things that weren't previously possible.
  • Our flexibility and dedication to the client ensure a superior custom-made and error-free product within a limited time frame.

Data Logging

With IoT and advanced data-logging techniques, Equonix has been at the forefront for providing products for next generation data logging. Our data logging comes as Standalone, PC based, Web based and Wireless. IoT products like sensors, microcontrollers, and wireless integrated circuits have helped us custom create impeccable, cost effective and highly efficient data loggers.

Advantages of Equonix Data Logging

  • With the most advanced techniques, we create data loggers which are easy to understand and simple to use and can be used by non engineers too.
  • We provide excellent documentation and data management in real time, alongwith carefully designed dashboards.
  • We help to provision, monitor and control your IoT entities securely, using rich server-side APIs. We help define relations between your devices, assets, customers or any other entities.
  • Our data loggers are designed to grow clients' businesses and convert data into valuable insights.
  • We create data loggers that improve operational efficiency, which can be turned into value, improvements, and substantial cost savings.
