Firmware

Development

At Equonix, we specialize in firmware and embedded software design and offer a wide range of custom embedded services to suit every requirement. We have a specialized team of firmware and embedded software engineers with extensive experience in Embedded-C language and expertise in Firmware development for high end processors and applications. We work with high-level and low-level firmware (ROM, OTP/PROM and PLA structures) and subsystems to power a variety of embedded systems.

  • 02
    We listen, discuss and then advise as we collaborate with you every step of the way, striving to meet your requirements, delivering on time and within your budget.
  • 03
    Our firmware development team is adept at product sustenance, lifecycle enhancements, testing and verification.
  • 04
    With our extensive R&D practice in embedded solutions and firmware development, and our proven project management processes, we build powerful electronic solutions, devices, and low-level components that enable innovation.

Hardware Design

At Equonix, we provide end to end services in hardware design and development from concept block diagram through circuit to high speed board design and support during manufacturing. We ensure that top grade and future ready processors, microcontrollers and other hardware components are used for creating clients products. Our hardware team of engineers is well versed with high speed micro controller design, latest Digital Signal Processing (DSPs) and Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs).

Advantages of Equonix Hardware Design

  • Our team of hardware experts handpick the most suitable components that go into manufacturing, from the processor platforms to the microcontrollers.
  • We deliver top notch embedded hardware solutions which can be scalable in near future, and meet every requirement of the client.
  • We make sure we help our clients with everything from production designs, power optimisation, certification support as well as manufacturing support with our partner ecosystem.
  • Our hardware designs conform to the stringent requirements of RTCA-DO-254 and RTCA-DO-160G standards.
  • We meet every challenge and every expectation of our clients and deliver the best in class hardware solutions.

System Testing

Equonix has a team of highly skilled embedded test engineers who have experience of executing complex projects by immediately adapting to the test setups and functional knowledge. We provide embedded software testing services across a range of target devices, Real Time Operating Systems (RTOS), development platforms and programming languages. Our testing services consist of code-level testing, as well as coverage analysis, functional testing, stress testing, code review, debugging, code maintenance and hardware sustainance.

Advantages of Equonix System Testing

  • We have a wide range of embedded testing services across Real Time Operating Systems (RTOS), development platforms and programming languages.
  • Our skilled team of experts have extensive experience to carry out the most complex embedded testing processes.
  • We have a holistic approach and also provide services like code reviews, documentation and code maintenance.
  • Our testing services are powered by robust tools and industry standard software testing frameworks like TMap Next, TPI/QBP.
  • While providing a reliable partnership, we help clients make a quality transformation and surpass their own software and hardware quality benchmarks.

Lifecycle Management

At Equonix, we offer structured consistent lifecycle management services, ALM (Application Lifestyle Management) and PLM (Product Lifestyle Management), for embedded hardware and software content, with a user-friendly approach. We work closely with our customers and deliver support including locked BOMs (Bill of Materials), locked hardware and firmware revisions, End-of-Life (EOL) notification, and extended inventory availability. From start to end, we automate and manage a company's needs by providing progress and accountability through reports and analysis and help to increase communications and productivity.

Advantages of Equonix Lifecycle Management

  • With over 10 years of experience, we have a unique background in delivering end-to-end lifecycle management using a range of services.
  • We have a team of skilled professionals with industry background for defining functional process definitions and supporting lifestyle management implementation, customization, administration and support.
  • We develop an effective project framework and a well constructed methodology of deployment by studying our clients' business processes, which ensure timely and effective delivery.
